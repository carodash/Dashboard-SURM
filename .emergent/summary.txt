<product_requirements>
The user, an open innovation manager, required a dashboard to manage potential (sourcing) and proven (dealflow) partners, replacing an unmanageable Excel sheet. The core problem was a lack of integrated data, difficulty in generating statistics, and manual data duplication.

Initial requirements:
- **Sourcing Management**: Capture company name, status, country, domain, typology, object, use case, technology, source, entry date, interest, presentation date, pilot, actions/comments.
- **Dealflow Management**: Capture name, status, domain, typology, object, source, pilot, relevant departments, reception date, pre-qualification date, meetup presentation date, business presentation date, Go for study date, Go for experimentation date, Go for generalization date, closure date, actions/comments, intermediate milestones.
- **Statistics**: Number of quarterly entries by domain, monthly pre-qualifications, Go for study counts, distribution by domain, typology, technology, and relevant departments.
- **Workflow**: Automated transition of pre-qualified startups from Sourcing to Dealflow without manual re-entry.

Subsequent additions requested and implemented:
- **Dashboard Customization**: Ability to add/remove fields and configure dashboard parameters.
- **Automated Web Data Enrichment**: Fetch company information (LinkedIn, funding rounds, creation year, contacts) from the web.
- **Search and Sorting**: Global search bar and sortable columns (ascending/descending).
- **Column Customization**: Ability to choose which columns to display on the dashboard.
- **Advanced Features**: Quick transition button (sourcing ↔ dealflow), user roles with granular permissions, and advanced filters.
- **Scoring and Prioritization**: Addition of a maturity/strategic interest score (e.g., 1-5 stars) and a high priority tag.
- **Predefined Domains**: A specific list of 23 domains (e.g., CleanTech, FinTech) for data consistency, with an Other option.
</product_requirements>
