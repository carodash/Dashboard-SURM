<product_requirements>
The primary objective was to replace an inefficient Excel-based system with a comprehensive Start-Up Relationship Management (SURM) dashboard. The core problem revolved around fragmented data, manual duplication, and a lack of analytical capabilities.

**Initial Core Requirements:**
*   **Sourcing & Dealflow Management:** Detailed capture of partner information, including company name, status, country, domain, typology, object, use case, technology, source, dates (entry, presentation, pilot), and comments.
*   **Workflow Automation:** Seamless transition of pre-qualified startups from Sourcing to Dealflow to prevent data re-entry.
*   **Statistics & Reporting:** Generation of key metrics such as quarterly entries by domain, monthly pre-qualifications, 'Go for study' counts, and distribution analysis by domain, typology, technology, and relevant departments.

**Subsequent Feature Enhancements (Implemented):**
*   **Dashboard Customization:** User-configurable fields and parameters.
*   **Automated Data Enrichment:** Web scraping for company details (LinkedIn, funding, contacts).
*   **Search & Sorting:** Global search and sortable table columns.
*   **Column Customization:** User-selected column visibility.
*   **Advanced Features:** Quick transition buttons, granular user roles (Admin, Manager, User), and advanced filtering.
*   **Scoring & Prioritization:** Maturity score (1-5 stars), strategic priority tags, potential score, and free-text strategic tags.
*   **Predefined Domains:** Enforced consistency with 23 specific domains plus Other.

**New Feature Requests (Current Phase - UX, Navigation & Visual Reporting):**
*   **Kanban View:** Visual pipeline with stages (Sourcing to Closure), showing key info, and supporting drag-and-drop.
*   **Synthetic Tables & Exports:** Cross-tabulations (by status, pilot, collaboration type) with PDF/CSV export.
*   **Quick Navigation:** Fixed tabs/shortcuts for My Startups, To Follow-up, With Docs, In Experimentation, and a global search.
*   **Mobile Experience:** Optimized responsive design, compact columns, reordered action buttons, card-based display.
*   **Future Customization:** Provision for custom fields, free tags, and strategic groups.

**Current Issues Reported by User:**
*   Kanban horizontal scrolling issue on standard screens.
*   Dense navigation bar with quick view buttons.
*   Non-functional Drag & Drop in Kanban.
</product_requirements>
